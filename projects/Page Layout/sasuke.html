The Story of Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is one of the last surviving members of the Uchiha Clan. His entire clan was wiped out by his older brother, Itachi Uchiha, which led Sasuke to swear revenge against him. Throughout his journey, Sasuke has been driven by his quest for power and vengeance, often walking a dark path to achieve his goals.
