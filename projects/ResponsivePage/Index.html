Gumball is the blue cat who always finds himself in wild and crazy situations...

Originally Gumball's pet goldfish, Darwin grew legs and became part of the family. He's loyal, caring, and sometimes wiser than his brother, helping Gumball through tough times.

Nicole is the backbone of the Watterson family. As a hardworking mother, she's fiercely protective and always ready to defend her family while juggling her career and home life.

Anais, the little pink bunny, is the most intelligent member of the Watterson family. Even at a young age, she often solves problems that stump her older siblings.

Richard, the big pink rabbit, is the playful but lazy dad of the family. He's always up for snacks and fun but tends to avoid responsibility when he can.
