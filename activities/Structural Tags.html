What goes in Main?

The "main" element contains the primary content of a webpage, such as articles, sections, and forms directly related to the page's main topic. It can also include relevant images or media that enhance this content. This structure helps organize information clearly, making it easily accessible for users and search engines.


What goes in the Footer?

The "footer" element typically contains information such as copyright details, navigation links to important pages (like privacy policy and terms of service), and contact information. It may also include links to social media profiles and credits for contributors.
