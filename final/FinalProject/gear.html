Essential Basketball Gear

Explore the must-have equipment for any basketball player, whether you're a beginner or a pro.

1. Basketballs

Basketballs come in various sizes and materials, designed for indoor or outdoor play. Official NBA basketballs are size 7 and made of leather.

2. Shoes

Basketball shoes provide ankle support and grip for quick movements on the court. Popular brands include Nike, Adidas, and Under Armour.

3. Jerseys and Apparel

Comfortable, breathable jerseys and shorts are essential for performance. Many players also wear compression gear for added support.

4. Accessories

  • Wristbands and headbands for sweat control
  • Knee pads for protection
  • Water bottles to stay hydrated
