Basketball Strategies

Understanding strategies is key to success in basketball. Here are some popular offensive and defensive plays:

Offensive Plays

  • Pick-and-Roll: A two-player play where one sets a screen for the other.
  • Fast Break: Quickly transitioning from defense to offense to score points.

Defensive Plays

  • Man-to-Man: Each defender guards a specific player.
  • Zone Defense: Defenders guard areas of the court instead of specific players.
